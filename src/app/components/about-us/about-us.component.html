ABOUT US

LSE MISSION

LightSpeed Entertainment, Inc. is an established Boston resource for arts that inspire come-unity. We specialize in events, initiatives and services that produce ideal environments for businesses, families and community members to form bonds. Our dream is to promote the inner beauty of our communities, strengthen relationships and support our collective health through the power of art, entertainment and love.

LSE LEGACY

LightSpeed Entertainment is an established resource for entertainment and media in Boston. Since 2008 LightSpeed has been a leading force producing trend setting media, events and educational programming that has greatly impacted the social and cultural landscape of Boston. Explore our new site and get connected to some of the great work we offer the community.

LSE 2020 VISION

Lightspeed is a resource for arts that inspire community. We use the power of art to promote culture, “love” and “light”. Our work builds community, inspires healthy lifestyles and supports the business of art. Our service model is currently under construction but please explore this abbreviated site and discover the great people, creativity and resources we have to offer. Love and Light."
